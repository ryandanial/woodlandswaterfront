WOODLANDS WATERFRONT PARK

A beautiful and peaceful coastal park providing an extraordinary view of the ocean from the north,
Woodlands Waterfront may be a top choice among residents and visitors of all ages.
The 11-hectare park houses a huge playing area with a wide variety of equipment and a 1.5-kilometre-long waterfront promenade,
making it a perfect spot for recreational exercises!

THINGS TO DO

BACK TO TOP

Exercise & Workout:
If you’re looking to workout or train for your IPPT that is coming soon, you can make use of the fitness corner that is equipped with facilities that can assist you in your training.


Cycling:
Cycling is an activity that countless of visitors love to do at Woodlands Waterfront Park. Bring your own bicycle together with your family here and have fun in the park.


Photography:
For those who love taking pictures, come here to enjoy the beautiful view of the Johor Straits, calming sunrise/sunset glow, flora and fauna or take candid pictures!


Fishing:
Bring your fishing lines and friends along here if you’re interested in fishing. Do take note of the fishing etiquette board that is placed near the entrance of the jetty.


Play:
If you’re bringing your kids over the park, they can enjoy the playground that has been placed few minutes away from the entrance. Do monitor your kids!


Dine-in:
After a long day of activities, enjoy a satisfying meal out with friends and family at Rasa Istimewa Waterfront Restaurant. And yes, it is halal-certified too!

Opening hours: 12PM - 12AM DAILY

Contact: 6366 9339

Address:6A Admiralty Road West, Woodlands Waterfront Park, Singapore 757445

It is about a 100-metre stretch of flat, concretised pavement. This is the place for you to lay your cutleries and goodies for a buffet if you intend to have one.

Green Lawn:
This piece of 700m2 verdant is the perfect spot to hold concerts/performances; guests can bring along their own mats for picnics while enjoying the shows.

Size 700 sqm

Seating capacity: 250 pax

Booking Fee: Free
